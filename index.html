Common Questions

Frequently asked

Answers to what most importers ask before working with us.

What products do you source?

We source a wide range of products including textiles, rice and agricultural goods, industrial equipment, consumer goods, and construction materials — primarily from Pakistan and South Asia.

Do you verify suppliers?

Yes — always. Supplier verification is central to everything we do. We screen every supplier for legitimacy, production capacity, quality compliance, and pricing reliability before presenting them to you.

Do you help with samples?

Absolutely. We manage the full sample process — from drafting the specification and coordinating with the supplier, to arranging delivery and managing quality feedback between both parties.

Which countries do you work with?

We primarily source from Pakistan, with connections across South Asia. We work with importers globally — Europe, GCC, North America, and beyond.

How do clients contact you?

WhatsApp is our primary channel for speed and directness. You can also reach us by email or through the enquiry form below. All enquiries are handled personally and promptly.

What makes Journey Trades different?

No layers of middlemen. No vague promises. Direct, accountable sourcing with structured communication and transparent execution — from day one to delivery.
