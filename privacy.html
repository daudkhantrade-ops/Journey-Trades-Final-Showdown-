1. Who We Are

Journey Trades is a sourcing and trade coordination agency based in Pakistan, operating internationally. We provide services to importers and businesses seeking supplier connections, procurement coordination, and trade support.

For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, "we", "us", and "Journey Trades" refers to the business entity operating under that name. "You" refers to any individual who visits our website, submits an enquiry, or communicates with us directly.

This website does not operate user accounts, e-commerce transactions, or subscription services. It is a static business website used for informational purposes and enquiry intake.

2. What Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

When you submit an enquiry through our contact form, the following information may be collected:

  • Full name
  • Company or business name
  • Email address
  • Country of operation
  • Product or sourcing requirements
  • Estimated quantities or budget range
  • Any additional details included in your message

Information Shared via WhatsApp or Email

If you contact us directly via WhatsApp or email, the content of those communications — including your contact details, product requirements, and any files shared — is received and stored by us for the purpose of responding to and fulfilling your enquiry.

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, basic technical data may be collected automatically, including:

  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring URL
  • Pages visited and time spent on the site
  • Approximate geographic location (country/region level, via IP address)

This data is collected in aggregate and is not used to personally identify individual visitors.

3. How We Collect It

We collect information through the following channels:

  • Contact Form: Submissions are processed via FormSubmit (formsubmit.co), a third-party form forwarding service that delivers enquiry data directly to our email inbox. FormSubmit may temporarily process submitted data on its servers.
  • Email: Direct email correspondence sent to our Gmail business address.
  • WhatsApp: Direct messages sent to our business WhatsApp number. These communications are subject to WhatsApp's own Privacy Policy in addition to ours.
  • Analytics: We may use Vercel Analytics or similar lightweight tools to understand general website traffic patterns. These tools do not collect personally identifiable information.

4. How We Use Your Information

Journey Trades uses the information we collect solely for legitimate business purposes:

  • Responding to your sourcing enquiry and understanding your requirements
  • Identifying suitable suppliers and coordinating on your behalf
  • Communicating service updates, timelines, and relevant sourcing information
  • Improving our website and service based on aggregate usage patterns
  • Maintaining records of communications for operational continuity

We do not use your information for unsolicited marketing, advertising, or any purpose beyond what is described above. We do not sell your information. Ever.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

Journey Trades does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information with any third party.

Your information may be shared in limited circumstances:

  • Suppliers: When coordinating sourcing on your behalf, we may share relevant product requirements (not personal contact details) with shortlisted suppliers. This is done only with your implied or explicit consent as part of the service.
  • FormSubmit: Our contact form uses FormSubmit to relay enquiries to our inbox. FormSubmit processes form submissions as a technical intermediary. Their privacy policy is available at formsubmit.co.
  • Vercel: Our website is hosted on Vercel. As a hosting provider, Vercel may process basic request data. Their privacy policy is available at vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information where required by applicable law, court order, or regulatory authority.

In all cases, information is shared only to the minimum extent necessary.

6. Cookies & Analytics

Our website is a static site with minimal tracking. We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or invasive tracking technologies.

We may use lightweight analytics tools (such as Vercel Analytics) that collect anonymised, aggregate data on site visits — page views, session duration, and referral sources. These tools are designed to be privacy-respecting and do not build individual user profiles.

Our contact form (via FormSubmit) may set a basic session cookie to manage form submission state. This cookie contains no personal information and expires at the end of your browser session.

If you prefer to browse without any cookies, you may adjust your browser settings accordingly. This will not materially affect your ability to use this website.

7. Data Retention

We retain enquiry and communication data for as long as it is operationally relevant — typically for the duration of an active engagement plus a reasonable period thereafter for reference and continuity purposes.

If an enquiry does not result in an active engagement, basic contact information may be retained for up to 12 months in case you wish to re-engage. After this period, it is deleted from our active records.

Email and WhatsApp communications are retained in our business accounts subject to those platforms' own data retention policies.

8. Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information you share with us. Our website operates over HTTPS. Business communications are handled through secured email and messaging platforms.

However, no method of internet transmission or electronic storage is completely secure. While we work hard to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security and accept no liability for unauthorised access beyond our reasonable control.

We encourage you to exercise care when sharing sensitive commercial information over any digital channel.

9. International Users

Journey Trades serves clients across multiple regions including Europe, the Gulf Cooperation Council, North America, and other international markets. This website is operated from Pakistan.

By using our website or submitting an enquiry from outside Pakistan, you understand that your information will be processed in Pakistan and potentially in the countries where our hosting and third-party infrastructure providers operate.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or another jurisdiction with specific data protection regulations, you retain all rights afforded to you under applicable law. We will honour data access and deletion requests regardless of your location.

10. Your Rights

Regardless of where you are located, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a summary of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Ask us to correct inaccurate or outdated information
  • Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information from our records
  • Objection: Object to the use of your information for any purpose beyond the core service
  • Portability: Request a copy of your information in a readable format

To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details in Section 14. We will respond within 30 days.

11. Requesting Data Deletion

To request deletion of your information from our records, please email us at journeytradesbusiness@gmail.com with the subject line: "Data Deletion Request".

Please include your name, company (if applicable), and the email address you used to contact us. We will confirm deletion within 30 days, subject to any legal retention obligations that may apply.

Please note: we cannot delete information from third-party platforms (such as WhatsApp or Gmail) on your behalf. For those, please refer to the respective platform's data management tools.

12. Children's Privacy

Journey Trades provides business-to-business services and this website is intended solely for use by adults engaged in commercial activity. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has submitted information through our website, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

13. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, services, or legal requirements. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent revision.

We encourage you to review this page periodically. Continued use of our website or services following an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
