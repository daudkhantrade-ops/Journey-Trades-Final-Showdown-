2. Description of Services

Journey Trades is a sourcing and trade coordination agency based in Pakistan. We assist international importers and businesses by providing the following services:

  • Identifying and connecting clients with potential suppliers in South Asia and beyond
  • Coordinating supplier communications on behalf of clients
  • Assisting with sample requests, specification communication, and product feedback
  • Supporting price negotiation processes between clients and suppliers
  • Providing trade coordination, export follow-up, and documentation assistance
  • General procurement support and operational sourcing advisory

Our role is that of a coordination and facilitation intermediary. We act as a professional bridge between buyers and suppliers — we do not manufacture, warehouse, ship, or take legal title to any goods.
